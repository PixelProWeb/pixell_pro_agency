✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +

Simply choose your package above, complete the payment via bKash or bank transfer, fill in the order form with your doctor and chamber details, then click "Submit Order via WhatsApp." Your complete order will open in WhatsApp automatically.

How do I submit payment proof? +

After submitting your order form, a WhatsApp message with your order details will open. You can send your payment screenshot directly in that WhatsApp conversation. Always save your transaction ID before submitting.

How long does the setup take? +

Facebook Page Setup (Package 01) typically takes 2–3 business days. Google Business Profile (Package 02) takes 3–5 days depending on Google's verification process. Monthly management packages (03 & 04) begin within 24 hours of order confirmation.

How does Google Business verification work? +

Google typically verifies business profiles by sending a postcard with a verification code to your chamber address, or via phone/video verification. We guide you through the entire process and assist with document preparation to make verification smooth and fast.

How do I send doctor photos and documents? +

You can upload photos directly in the order form above. After submitting, you can also send additional high-resolution photos via WhatsApp to 01936474743. Please provide clear, professional photos of the doctor and chamber for best results.
